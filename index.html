About North East Pride Cup

North East Pride Cup

The North East Pride Cup is an amateur tournament for players under the categories of 14 and 16 in the 7 North Eastern States of India.

The purpose of the tournament is to encourage football in the region in a safe and structured environment, while also developing psychosocial qualities like sportsmanship, team spirit, and leadership.

To mark and celebrate the centenary year of Don Bosco in India, 19 zone wise tournaments will be conducted in two phases across the North East India region wherein the schools and academies in each city can send their teams to participate.

The best teams from each tournament (per zone) will get a chance to come for Finale in Guwahati city and fight for the NE Pride Cup.
